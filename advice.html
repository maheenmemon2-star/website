Practical Travel Advice for Romania

Planning a trip to Romania? Here's everything you need to know to make your journey smooth, enjoyable, and memorable. This practical guide covers the best times to visit, transportation options, and tips I wish I'd known before my trip.

Best Time to Travel

Seasonal Breakdown

Spring

Weather: 15-20 degrees

Pros: Less tourists, better weather.

Cons: It might rain often

Best for: Visiting cities

Summer

Weather: 30-40 degrees

Pros: Sunny

Cons: Intense heat, lots of tourists

Best for: Going out during the evening

Autumn

Weather:20-25 degrees

Pros: Less crowded

Cons: Cold nights and hot days

Best for: Visiting nature

Winter

Weather: -5 - 5 degrees

Pros: Lots of snowfall

Cons: Very cold

Best for:Explore Christmas markets

My Recommendation

Best overall time to visit:

In my experience, the best time to visit is during Spring, maybe around April/May. There aren't many tourists and you can still benefit from good weather without all the negative aspects.

Essential Tips & Practical Information

Money & Budget

Currency: Romanian leu (1 euro = aprox. 4.97 RON

Cash vs Card: Both are accepted everywhere

Daily Budget Estimates

  • Budget Traveler: 20 euros = 100 RON
  • Mid-Range: 40 euros = 200 RON
  • Luxury: 100 euro = 500 RON

Practical Essentials

Before You Go

  • Visa Requirements: Romania is part of the European Union, so it accepts any European passport
  • Power Plugs:Contrary to Irish power plugs, Romania uses the European standard plug

Final Words of Advice

Choose the season you want to travel during based on your travel preferences (nature, exploring cities, staying inside etc.)
