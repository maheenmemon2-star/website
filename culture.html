Culture & Lifestyle in Romania

Romanian culture is heavily influenced by Slavic and Balkanic culture.

Food & Cuisine

Sarmale

Ground meat tightly wrapped inside of cabbage.

Where to try it: Every restaurent offers it

Cozonac

Traditional cake that is usually served during Christmas

Where to try it: Can be bought anywhere, or even homemade.

Plescavita

Originally from Serbia, it is a very popular street food in Romania

Where to try it: You will find little food trucks selling them in any Romanian city

Music, Festivals & Traditions

UNTOLD music festival

The 3rd biggest festival in the world. It's an electronic music festival that takes place every year during the summer in the city of Cluj, in Transylvania.

Christmas caroling tradition

Every year, a few days before Christmas, children will get together and go to every house in their neighborhood singing Christmas carols, expecting sweets or money in return.

Social Customs & Etiquette

How People Greet Each Other

People will usually just shake hands. Men will kiss the hand of older women.

Dress Code & Appearance

There is no specific dress code or mandatory appearance.

What to Wear

  • In cities:Anything you would usually wear
  • At religious sites:If you are a woman, you should cover up your body.
  • At the beach/resort:Anything you would usually wear
  • For formal occasions:Anything you would usually wear
