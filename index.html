This website is aimed at people who love to travel and explore new places. We hope that you will find information on this site useful when planning your next trip abroad.We hope that you enjoy exploring Europe with us, with honest reviews of each country!

Welcome to Europe!

Europe is a continent that has many different cultures and languages. It's also known as the "Old Continent". The European Union (EU) is an economic and political union of 27 member states. There are more than 40 official languages spoken across Europe.

There are many countries in Europe such as France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Greece, Switzerland, Austria, Poland, Romania etc. Each country has its own culture and language. For example, French people speak French while Germans speak German. In some cases there can be multiple languages spoken within one country like in Belgium which speaks Dutch,French and German.

Some popular cities include Paris, Brussels, Rome, Madrid, Barcelona and Berlin. These cities have lots of tourist attractions including museums, monuments and parks.

Overall, Europe offers something for everyone whether it’s history or modern day life.
