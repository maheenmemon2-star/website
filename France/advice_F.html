Advice for Future Travellers

Essential tips and recommendations for your visit to France

Travel Tips

Best Time to Travel

April to June (Spring) - comfortable weather, fewer crowds. September to October - often cheaper flights, calmer streets.

Transport

Use the Paris Metro - fast and inexpensive. Getting around was easy thanks to the Metro. Many signs are in French, but staff usually understand basic English.

What I'd Do Differently

Visit southern France - especially Nice, Cannes, or Marseille - for beaches and coastal culture.
