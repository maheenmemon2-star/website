Places to Visit

Discover the most iconic destinations in France

Top Attractions

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is one of the most iconic structures in the world. Built for the 1889 World's Fair, it stands at 330 meters tall. Visitors can go up the tower and view Paris from multiple levels. The Eiffel Tower at night was the highlight with hourly sparkles.

Louvre Museum

One of the largest and most famous art museums in the world. It is home to over 35,000 artworks, including the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Palace of Versailles

A historic royal palace known for its massive gardens, the Hall of Mirrors, and architecture from the time of Louis XIV.
