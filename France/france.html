Overview

France is a major European destination known for culture, architecture, fashion, and food. It borders Belgium, Germany, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, and more. I visited France in 2022 during the summer holidays with my family. It was my first time in France, and the atmosphere of the city, especially in the evenings stood out to me. The architecture felt historic, the streets were full of people from all over the world, and every corner seemed to have a cafe. The trip helped me understand why Paris is often described as the "City of Light."
