A Belgian waffle a day keeps the doctor away.

Best time to travel

Christmas

I believe the best time to travel is at the end of November, mainly because of the Christmas markets. Around this time, the whole city starts to take on a warm, festive atmosphere, with lights, decorations, and seasonal foods everywhere. I also think the city would look especially aesthetic in the winter, creating the perfect backdrop for exploring, taking photos, and enjoying cosy moments. Overall, it would make for a wonderful holiday just before Christmas, givinga chance to relax, soak up the festive spirit, and start the season in a really magical way.

Transportation

Taxis

I think that taxis are the best option because using the bus might be a bit difficult for travellers. The buses donâ€™t go to every location, so even after getting off, you would often need to take a train to reach your main destination. However, the train system can be even more challenging for new travellers, especially for a big family trying to stay together and navigate unfamiliar routes. For these reasons, taxis offer a simpler, more convenient way to get around.

What I would do differently

What I would do differently is stay for a longer period of time and explore other cities in Belgium because I think I would really enjoy it. I heard that Bruge is a really pretty city, you could go on a boat ride on a river, so I would love to explore that.
