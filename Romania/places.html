Must-Visit Places in Romania

Romania offers an incredible variety of destinations for every type of traveller. Here are some of the most remarkable places you should consider visiting.

1. The city of Brasov

About This Place

The city of Brasov, located in the Transylvania region, is in my opinion the most beautiful city in Romania. It has picturesque, old, cobblestone streets, an impressive cathedral and many more. Because it is located in the heart of the Carpathian mountains, it offers spectacular views all year round. Brasov has a very warm and welcoming atmosphere to it, making it the second most visited city in the country.

What to Do

  • Brasov Christmas market
  • Bear Watching
  • Day trip to Dracula's castle

Best Time to Visit

In my opinion, it's best to visit Brasov during Winter. There is a lot of snow over the mountains and you can even go skiing. There is also a very famous Christams market that attracts lots of tourists.

2. Bucharest

About This Place

The capital and most populated city in Romania.

What to Do

  • Stavropolos monastery, built in 1724.
  • Terme bucharest, Europe's largest wellnes & relaxation centre.
  • Communism Tour. Re-experience daily life under communism rule

Best Time to Visit

Anytime but the summer, as it gets extremely hot.

3. Sibiu

About This Place

This 12th century medieval town offers incredible views

What to Do

  • Explore the old town.
  • Visit the 15th century Corvin Castle.
  • Explore the markets in the main square.

Best Time to Visit

From April to June, the temperature is just perfect for exploring the city.
