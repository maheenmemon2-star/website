My Personal Experience in [Country Name]

Traveling to Romania every year is always very exciting. Here's an honest account of my journey this summer, including the highlights, challenges, and everything in between.

What I Enjoyed Most

Food

One of the aspects I love most about Romania is the food. It's literally impossible to find bad restaurants anywhere. The people there put so much care and effort into the food and that's what makes it worthwhile.

Family

I also visited my grandparents and my cousins. I'm always very happy to see them because I don't visit them very often.

Nature

Romanian nature is very impressive. I went hiking in the Carpathian mountains one day, and was at the beach the other!

Challenges & what I Enjoyed Least

The weather

Romanian weather during peak summer season is not to be taken lightly. It was almost impossible to stand outside between 10am to 6pm because of the scorching heat.

Crowded beaches

Many romanian people decidde to go to the beach during summer and it'a always extremely crowded

Communication Experience

Pretty much everyone there speaks English well enough to get by, but try learning some romanian words to impress the locals!

Overall Rating & Recommendation

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Lots of things to do
  • Beautiful nature
  • Very good food
  • Friendly people

Cons

  • Don't go during the summer
  • Some tourist attractions can be overly expensive
  • lack of public transport

Would I Recommend It?

I would recommend Romania as a travel destination for anyone who is looking for a hidden gem in Europe. It's definetely worth visitng at least once.
